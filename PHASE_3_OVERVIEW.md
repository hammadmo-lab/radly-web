## Key Success Factors

### 1. **Content Quality**
- 1,500-2,000 words minimum
- Original research/data where possible
- Actionable advice (not just theory)
- Proper internal linking

### 2. **Consistency**
- 2-3 posts per week minimum
- Regular publishing schedule
- Long-term commitment (6+ months)
- Ongoing promotion

### 3. **Link Building**
- Quality over quantity
- Relevant sources (healthcare, medical tech)
- Diverse link sources
- Natural anchor text

### 4. **Monitoring**
- Weekly check of rankings
- Monthly analysis of traffic
- Quarterly strategy reviews
- Optimization based on data

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need to hire writers?**
A: Not required, but recommended if you want consistent output. 2-3 internal writers can handle this, or use freelancers for parts.

**Q: How much does Phase 3 cost?**
A: $200-1,500 minimum (press releases + directories). Optional costs: writers ($4-8k/month), agency ($3-10k/month).

**Q: When will I see results?**
A: First impressions in week 2, ranking improvements by week 4, significant traffic by week 8+.

**Q: Do I need to do all 7 tasks?**
A: Blog + link building are critical. FAQ and author markup are nice-to-have. Focus on blog first, add others after launch.

**Q: What if we can only write 1 post per month?**
A: Slower growth, but still effective. Stretch Phase 3 to 12+ weeks, adjust expectations accordingly.

**Q: Can we do Phase 3 in parallel with Phase 2?**
A: Not recommended. Phase 2 should be submitted to Search Console first. Phase 3 starts Week 2-3 of Phase 2 monitoring.
